About Ofure Orphanage

A sanctuary built on faith, compassion, and a divine calling to provide a loving home for vulnerable children.

Our Story

A Divine Calling

Ofure Orphanage was established following a divine call to "Go out there, gather the children, shelter and feed them." What began as a response to this spiritual directive has grown into a sanctuary of hope for vulnerable children.

For over 10 years, we have been committed to providing not just a home, but a foundation of faith, discipline, and love that empowers children to overcome their circumstances and thrive.

Our name "Ofure" reflects our mission to offer a place where children can find healing, purpose, and the opportunity to grow into their full potential as contributing members of society.

Our Founder

Matron Philomena Isiramen

Founder & Director, Ofure Clinic

Matron Philomena Isiramen founded Ofure Orphanage with a vision to create a nurturing environment where vulnerable children could find not only shelter and sustenance but also spiritual guidance and emotional support.

Her background in healthcare as the Director of Ofure Clinic provided her with unique insights into the holistic needs of children, addressing both their physical wellbeing and spiritual development.

"Following a divine call to 'Go out there, gather the children, shelter and feed them,' we established Ofure Orphanage to provide not just a home, but a foundation of faith and discipline."

Under her leadership, the orphanage has grown to become a beacon of hope, transforming the lives of countless children through compassionate care and unwavering commitment to their future.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Guided by faith, our mission is to gather vulnerable children, providing them with a loving, safe home, sustenance, and nurturing care. We aim to instill discipline and strong values, empowering them to reach their full potential as contributing members of society.

Our Vision

To raise God-fearing, disciplined, and resilient children who, despite their circumstances, grow to be the best in their community and contribute positively to the nation. We envision a future where every child under our care thrives.

Our Core Values

Faith & God-Fearing

Rooted in Christian principles and daily devotion, nurturing spiritual growth in all children.

Discipline & Character

Instilling strong values, resilience, and self-discipline to build character and integrity.

Love & Compassion

Providing nurturing care, understanding, and unconditional love to every child in our home.

Opportunity & Growth

Fostering potential through education, mentorship, and support for holistic development.
