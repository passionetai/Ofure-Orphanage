Our Home

Serving children for over 10 years

Matron Philomena Isiramen

Founder & Director, Ofure Clinic

"Following a divine call to 'Go out there, gather the children, shelter and feed them,' we established Ofure Orphanage to provide not just a home, but a foundation of faith and discipline."

Our Mission

Guided by faith, our mission is to gather vulnerable children, providing them with a loving, safe home, sustenance, and nurturing care. We aim to instill discipline and strong values, empowering them to reach their full potential as contributing members of society.

Our Vision

To raise God-fearing, disciplined, and resilient children who, despite their circumstances, grow to be the best in their community and contribute positively to the nation. We envision a future where every child under our care thrives.

Our Impact

Transforming Lives Through Faith-Based Care and Support Since 2014!

Safe Housing

Providing a secure, clean, and loving home environment where children feel safe and cherished, supported by dedicated caregivers.

Quality Education

Ensuring all children attend school, providing necessary supplies, and offering tutoring support to help them succeed academically.

Healthcare & Nutrition

Prioritizing the physical well-being of our children through regular check-ups, necessary medical care, and nutritious meals.

Spiritual & Emotional Support

Nurturing faith through daily devotion and prayer, alongside emotional support to help children build resilience and strong character.

Life Skills & Activities

Engaging children in constructive activities and teaching practical life skills to prepare them for independent futures.

Community Building

Fostering a sense of family within the orphanage and maintaining connections with the wider community and supporters.

Stories of Transformation

"When I first came to Ofure Orphanage three years ago, I struggled with reading and was falling behind in school. The tutors here spent extra time with me every evening, and now I'm one of the top students in my class. I've discovered my love for mathematics and dream of becoming an engineer someday."

JO

Joseph O.

Resident, Age 14

"After losing my parents, I felt so alone and angry at the world. The staff at Ofure Orphanage showed me patience and love when I needed it most. Through the daily devotions and prayer times, I've found peace and a sense of purpose. I now help the younger children adjust when they first arrive, sharing with them the same comfort I received."

GE

Grace E.

Resident, Age 16

"I've been volunteering at Ofure Orphanage for the past two years, teaching music once a week. The transformation I've witnessed in these children is nothing short of miraculous. Many arrive withdrawn and hesitant, but through the nurturing environment and faith-based approach, they blossom into confident, joyful individuals. What makes Ofure special is how they address not just physical needs, but spiritual and emotional healing as well."

DA

Dr. Adebayo

Volunteer Music Teacher
