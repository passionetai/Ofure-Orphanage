Our Impact

Transforming lives, building futures, and creating lasting change for vulnerable children in our community.

Making a Difference

For over 10 years, Ofure Orphanage has been committed to providing not just a home, but a foundation of faith, discipline, and love that empowers children to overcome their circumstances and thrive.

Safe Housing

We provide clean, comfortable dormitories with 24/7 caregiver support, creating a true home environment for our children where they feel safe, loved, and nurtured.

Quality Education

All school-aged children attend local schools with uniforms, supplies, and tutoring provided by our staff, ensuring they have the tools they need to succeed academically.

Healthcare

Regular medical check-ups, dental care, and nutrition programs ensure our children grow up healthy and strong, with access to the care they need to thrive.

Counseling

Our trained counselors help children process trauma and build resilience through individual and group therapy, providing emotional support and guidance.

Extracurriculars

Sports, arts, music, and vocational training programs help children discover their talents and passions, developing well-rounded individuals with diverse skills.

Community

We maintain connections with extended family when possible and facilitate adoption or foster care placements, ensuring children maintain important relationships.
